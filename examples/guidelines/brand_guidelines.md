# TechStyle Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Brand Name:** TechStyle  
**Tagline:** Innovation Meets Elegance  
**Brand Voice:** Bold, innovative, customer-focused, premium yet accessible

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Electric Blue:** #0066FF (Primary brand color - use for headlines, CTAs)
- **Midnight Black:** #1A1A1A (Secondary - use for body text, backgrounds)

### Secondary Colors
- **Cyber Teal:** #00D9C0 (Accent - use sparingly for highlights)
- **Sunset Orange:** #FF6B35 (Call-to-action accent)
- **Pure White:** #FFFFFF (Backgrounds, contrast)

## Typography

### Primary Font Family
**Montserrat**
- Headlines: Montserrat Bold (700)
- Subheadlines: Montserrat SemiBold (600)
- Body: Montserrat Regular (400)

### Secondary Font Family
**Open Sans**
- Supporting text
- Captions and annotations

### Text Overlay Settings
- **Drop Shadows:** Disabled (clean, modern aesthetic)
- Text should be clearly visible through color contrast, not shadows
- text_shadow: false
- flat 2D vector style
- Flat vector art

## Photography Style

### Guidelines
- **Clean, modern, minimalist** product photography
- **High-key lighting** with subtle shadows
- **Neutral backgrounds** (white, light gray)
- **Lifestyle shots** showing products in real-world contexts
- **Focus on product details** and premium quality

### Composition
- Center-aligned product placement
- Generous negative space
- Rule of thirds for lifestyle shots

## Logo Usage

### Clearspace
- Minimum clearspace: **20 pixels** on all sides
- Maintain proportions - never stretch or distort

### Minimum Size
- Digital: **50 pixels** width minimum
- Print: **0.5 inches** width minimum

### Placement
- Preferred: Top-left or bottom-right corner
- Always maintain clearspace
- Use on contrasting backgrounds only

## Prohibited Uses

- ❌ Never use gradients on the logo
- ❌ Do not rotate or skew brand elements
- ❌ Avoid cluttered backgrounds
- ❌ Never use non-brand colors for the logo
- ❌ Do not place text over busy imagery
- ❌ Do not use drop shadow on text

## Prohibited Visual Elements

- Dark, gloomy, or low-quality imagery
- Overly busy or cluttered compositions
- Competitor products or logos
- Dated or retro design styles
- Low-resolution or pixelated images

## Social Media Guidelines

### Instagram & Facebook
- Use 1:1 (square) format for posts
- 9:16 (portrait) for Stories and Reels
- Maintain brand colors and fonts
- Include product in every creative

### LinkedIn & Twitter
- 16:9 (landscape) format preferred
- Professional tone
- Focus on innovation and technology

## Approved Taglines

- "Innovation Meets Elegance"
- "Elevate Your Experience"
- "Premium Technology, Accessible Price"
- "Where Style Meets Performance"

## Tone & Voice

- **Bold:** Confident statements about product quality
- **Innovative:** Highlight cutting-edge features
- **Customer-Focused:** Emphasize benefits and user experience
- **Premium yet Accessible:** High quality without pretension

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
✅ Use high-quality product imagery  
✅ Maintain consistent brand colors  
✅ Apply generous white space  
✅ Emphasize product benefits  
✅ Use clear, bold headlines  

### Don't:
❌ Use stock photos that don't feature products  
❌ Mix fonts outside the brand family  
❌ Overcrowd designs with too many elements  
❌ Use discount/sale language (maintain premium positioning)  
❌ Apply heavy filters or effects
